What is a First Year Seminar?

  • Courses can range from 1-2 units either pass/no-pass or letter graded

  • First year seminars are courses open to first year students (with 45 units or less)

  • Each seminar has a 19 student limit to allow for small group discussions and the ability to work closely with your professor

Benefits to First Year Seminars

  • Utilize this excellent opportunity to meet new people and form connections

  • Introduce yourself to new topics without having a large course load

  • Gain hands on experience in various labs and outdoor experience
